Project type: End-to-end mobile app

Role: Sole designer

Industry: Industrial

Tools: Figma

Duration: Q3 2023

Client

The client is a crane manufacturer that needed a mobile application for crane operators to document daily inspections of lifting equipment. Previously, these inspections were done using paper and pen, a method that was inefficient and prone to errors.

Project

The goal was to digitize the inspection process, allowing operators to input data directly into a mobile app. These reports would then be exported as PDFs and forwarded to the responsible person at their workplace.

My role

Full UX/UI design process, from initial research to final implementation, and direct communication with the client to understand their requirements and ensure that the app met their needs.

Outcome

Delivered a mobile application that digitized the crane inspection process.
