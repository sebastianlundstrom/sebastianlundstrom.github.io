Green Urbansights

Project type: Web page

Role: Designer

Industry: Environmental management

Tools: Figma

Duration: Q2 2024

Client

Green Urbansights is a startup specializing in monitoring and maintaining trees in urban environments using satellite data and AI. As they were in the startup phase without an existing website, they needed an online presence to clearly communicate their mission and showcase their products.

Project

The goal was to design a website that was visually appealing, easy to navigate, and cost-effective. The website also needed to be easy for the client to maintain after handover, given their limited resources as a startup.

My role

Designed the website to align with the client’s mission and product offerings.

Suggest the best platform for publishing, based on the client's specific needs and preferences.
