Qvantum Energi AB

Project type: Desktop and mobile applications

Role: UX Designer

Industry: Heat Pump Manufacturing

Tools: Figma, Adobe XD

Duration: Q4 2023 - Q2 2024

Client

Qvantum has been a Swedish heat pump manufacturer for 30 years, initially specializing in custom-built industrial heat pumps. They have recently entered the consumer market with connected heat pumps designed for smart homes. The company’s goal is to provide their award-winning sustainable heat pump solutions for residential use.

Project

Qvantum needed a software tool that could remotely monitor and manage installed heat pumps to support their after-sales and support teams. This tool would help with installation, troubleshooting, and ongoing maintenance, improving overall customer service.

Design Process

Research: Conducted workshops and one-on-one interviews with support team members and managers to gather insights into their needs for remote support. Focused on understanding the pain points and requirements of the after-sales team, aiming to create a tool that would make troubleshooting and monitoring as easy as possible.

Documentation: Created high-level feature descriptions based on the research findings. Developed wireframes and interactive prototypes to visualize the user experience and key functionalities.

Collaboration: Worked with managers, users and the development team, providing mockups and feature descriptions to ensure the deliverables aligned with the design vision.

Delivered Solutions

High-Level Feature Descriptions: Defined essential features and functionalities for the remote support tool.

User Flows: For handling single and multiple heat pump installations.

Wireframes & Prototypes: Designed detailed wireframes and prototypes for the web-based support tool, consumer mobile app, and factory assembly software.

Outcome

A web-based software tool that enables the after-sales team to monitor, troubleshoot, and control multiple heat pumps remotely.

Heat pump information: Access detailed data on individual heat pumps.

Sensor graph comparison: View and compare real-time sensor graphs for multiple units.

Alarm trigger lists: Easily identify and troubleshoot issues using automated alarm triggers.

Saved heat pumps: Search for and temporarily save specific heat pumps for continuous monitoring over time.

This tool enhanced the after-sales team’s ability to provide fast and effective support to heat pump installers, leading to improved customer satisfaction and operational efficiency.
